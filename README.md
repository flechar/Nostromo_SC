# Proposal:  Nostromo Smart Contract Update

## 1. Overview
Our goal is to deliver a comprehensive Nostromo Smart Contract (SC) with a Project Hub that serves as the primary gateway for projects joining the Qubic ecosystem.

At its core, the platform provides a professional-grade Fundraising Engine. By evolving the initial Nostromo architecture, we are significantly enhancing the UX/UI and dynamics to meet current market standards. This includes removing outdated elements, such as the Tier System, which the community and Shareholders (SH) identified as a big barrier to the adoption of Nostromo.

To maximize ecosystem value, we are introducing three critical pillars:

**Multi-Modal Fundraising Engine:** The core of Nostromo SC.

**Auction House:** Providing a 'Price Discovery' mechanism that allows the market to define fair value for assets and projects.

**Governance:** A support for all the Nostromo ecosystem giving transparency, governance, a dispute system to ensure investor protection and long-term viability.

This approach goes beyond simply raising funds, creating a robust and comprehensive financial structure for launching and growing new projects.
  
**IMPORTANT:** The current proposal is a summarized version of the system. A more detailed description of the mechanisms and some examples can be found in **Chapter 11 (More Detailed Documentation #1**).

## 2. Business Value & Ecosystem Impact

The Nostromo evolution is designed to solve three critical gaps currently hindering the Qubic ecosystem's growth:

**Standardized Project Entry (The Project Hub):** By providing a professional, centralized "front door" for new projects, we reduce the friction for developers and investors to find each other, increasing the overall "stickiness" of the ecosystem.

**Market-Driven Liquidity (Price Discovery):** Current fundraising methods lack a fair mechanism to value assets. Our Auction House allows the community to define the market price for new project tokens and illiquid asset bundles, ensuring capital efficiency.

**Investor Protection & Trust:** The integrated Governance and Dispute system, ensures that fundraising is not just a "one-way street." By tying funds to milestone approvals and providing a mechanism to handle conflicts (e.g., impossible idea or empty ideas), we build the professional credibility required to attract serious capital to Qubic.

## 3. Multi-Modal Fundraising Engine
The core of the Nostromo SC  is a versatile fundraising engine designed to accommodate everything from community solidarity to professional-grade project launches.

### 3.1 Core Modules
**Donation Campaigns:** Optimized for non-profit and community support (e.g., event sponsorships) with a reduced fee structure to maximize social impact.

**Traditional IDOs:** A milestone-based fundraising model where projects must provide full transparency (Team, Tokenomics, Budget allocation). Funds are strictly released upon milestone completion to ensure accountability.

**Auction-Based IDOs (Batch Auctions):** Our flagship feature for high-tier projects. It utilizes batch auction logic to enable Price Discovery, ensuring that the market—not arbitrary fixed prices—defines the fair value of new assets.

### 3.2 Key Ecosystem Benefits
**Asset Bundling:** Allows the sale of a set of assets in a single auction, enabling the market to determine a fair price for the asset and increasing liquidity within Qubic.

**Investor Security:** IDOs are linked to a Shareholder Voting System for defining mandatory milestones and releasing funds earmarked for those milestones, mitigating risk to the community.

**Versatility:** Supports multiple fundraising dynamics, from fixed-price to competitive bidding, attracting a wider range of developers.

### 3.3 Economic Alignment (Distribution Structure)
The distribution model is designed to attract large-scale projects by offering volume-based fee reductions, directly benefiting Qubic Shareholders:

## 4. Auction House: Price Discovery & Liquidity Engine
The Auction House is the Project Hub’s strategic tool designed to resolve current gaps in asset liquidity and allow the market to define the fair value of new launches.

### 4.1 Auction Mechanisms
**Batch Auctions (Institutional Grade):** Optimized for large-scale IDOs where the seller defines the quantity and a floor price. Bids are ranked by value, ensuring assets are efficiently distributed to the participants who value them most.

**Standard Auctions (Asset Liquidity):** Focused on individual assets or "bundles" (mixed lots of tokens/shares). This model is vital for providing an exit for illiquid assets through competitive bidding with built-in anti-sniping protection.

### 4.2 Strategic Business Value
**Price Discovery:** Eliminates arbitrary pricing for new projects, allowing actual market demand to dictate value.

**Asset Bundling:** Facilitates a secondary market for project fractions or share sets, increasing the velocity of capital within the Qubic ecosystem.

**Buy Now & Private Auctions:** Some features offer the flexibility for private auctions or instant sales, generating additional fees for the ecosystem.

## 5. The Project Hub: Innovation Pipeline
The Project Hub acts as a structured incubator where project concepts are transformed into tradable, developable assets, ensuring a constant flow of new initiatives into the Qubic ecosystem.

### 5.1 Innovation Categories
**Project Suggestions (Open Source):** A non-commercial space for identifying ecosystem problems and proposing solutions to inspire the community.

**Project Ideas (Tradable Assets):** A formalized framework for creators to sell detailed project specifications to developers or investors. To ensure quality, sellers must stake 100% of the project's value as collateral.

### 5.2 Investor Protection & Quality Control
Addressing concerns regarding the quality of "sold ideas," the Hub implements a strict tiered access and dispute system:

**Tiered Discovery:** Information is released in stages (Title -> Introduction -> Full Specification), allowing buyers to assess the concept before the final 90% payment (to acquire the final stage).

**Collateralized Trust:** Sellers must "stack" the equivalent of the sale price. If a dispute (plagiarism, empty content, or impossible idea) is found valid by the Governance vote, the buyer is refunded and receives the seller's collateral as compensation.

**Governance Oversight:** Every sale is protected by a 24-hour dispute window, where Shareholders and the Manager act as a quality jury.

### 5.3 Sustainability Model (Fee Structure)
The Project Hub serves as a deflationary and revenue-generating tool for Qubic ecosystem:

**Creation Fees (Burned):** A 50M fee for Ideas and a 10M fee for Suggestions, directly reducing Qubic supply.

**Success Fees:** A 1.5% fee on all successful sales, ensuring the platform's long-term maintenance and alignment with the ecosystem's growth.

## 6. Ecosystem Governance & Investor Protection
To address the need for long-term viability and security, the Nostromo SC incorporates a streamlined governance framework focused on accountability and dispute resolution.

### 6.1 Governance Entities
**The Shareholders (Ultimate Authority):** As the primary decision-making body, Shareholders vote on approving project milestones, releasing funds related to those milestones, and resolving disputes.

**The Manager (Operational Oversight):** Responsible for day-to-day coordination, technical optimization, community management (Discord/X), ensuring the platform remains active and professional.

### 6.2 Investor Protection & Dispute System
The core business value of this governance model is the Dispute Mechanism, milestones approval, milestones funds release, which protects the community from "empty" or fraudulent projects, or malicius fundraisings:

**Milestone-Gated Funding:** Funds from Fundraisings are not released upfront but are tied to verified technical milestones approved by the Shareholders.

**Conflict Resolution:** In cases of "empty" projects ideas or failed ideas, a formal dispute can be opened, where Shareholders act as the jury to decide on refunds or penalties.

### 6.3 Strategic Objectives
**Transparency:** Every proposal follows a strict framework (Problem Statement, Proposed Changes) to ensure clarity for voters.

**Scalability:** Allows the Nostromo ecosystem to grow under structured oversight while maintaining decentralized principles.

**Risk Mitigation:** Shifts the risk away from the individual investor by providing a collective oversight layer managed by Nostromo Shareholders.
